// About the Game //

Welcome to MONKE PORTALS, a thrilling new VR game that throws you into the role of a hyper-intelligent primate test subject armed with revolutionary portal-generating gloves. Drawing inspiration from the fast-paced locomotion of Gorilla Tag and the mind-bending puzzles of the Portal series, MonkePortals offers a one-of-a-kind, physically-driven traversal experience where every leap, climb, and launch is governed by the unfiltered laws of motion and momentum.
